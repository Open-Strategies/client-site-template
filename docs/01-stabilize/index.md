# Stabilize

The first phase is about grounding. Before we can name patterns or build new structures, we create the conditions for people to show up as themselves — not just their stress responses.

**Coaching posture:** Slow, reflective, grounding.

**What this phase looks like:** Sessions focus on reducing emotional charge, building trust, and establishing the rhythm of the coaching relationship. The goal is not to solve anything yet — it's to create enough safety that honest reflection becomes possible.

**Moving on:** When affect settles and a person can observe their experience rather than relive it, they're ready for the next phase.
