# Sustain

The final phase anchors the work into identity. The structures and practices built in earlier phases become part of how a person leads — not something they're trying, but something they are.

**Coaching posture:** Stewardship; long-view.

**What this phase looks like:** Sessions take a longer view. We look at how the work has changed not just behavior but self-concept. Boundaries feel right rather than effortful. Decisions align with values without requiring constant deliberation.

**Completion indicators:** Boundaries feel natural. Values-aligned decisions happen without extended internal negotiation. The person can sustain their growth without the coaching structure.
