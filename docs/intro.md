## How to use this site

Navigate the sidebar to explore each phase. The phases reflect where the coaching work is headed, not a rigid timeline — people move through them based on readiness, not calendars.

The **Cohort** section holds individual profiles and session notes for each person in the coaching engagement. The **Tools & Assessments** section contains frameworks and instruments we use along the way.

> This site is yours. When the engagement concludes, you walk away with a complete, navigable record of the work — the insights, the tools, the progress. It belongs to you.
